6. **Hokkaido**: If you're visiting in winter, Hokkaido offers fantastic skiing and hot springs. Summer brings beautiful lavender fields.

7. **Onsen Experience**: Visit a traditional Japanese hot spring. Remember, most onsens have strict rules, including no tattoos in many public baths.

8. **Cultural Activities**: Try a tea ceremony, kimono experience, or learn calligraphy to immerse yourself in Japanese culture.

As for learning basic Japanese phrases quickly, here are a few tips:

- **Apps**: Apps like Duolingo, Memrise, or HelloTalk can be very helpful for beginners.
- **YouTube Channels**: Learn from channels like JapanesePod101 or Easy Japanese, which offer free lessons.
- **Phrasebooks**: Carry a phrasebook or use digital versions for quick reference.
- **Basic Phrases**: Start with greetings like "こんにちは" (konnichiwa, hello), "ありがとう" (arigatou, thank you), "すみません" (sumimasen, sorry/Excuse me), and numbers.
- **Practice Pronunciation**: Listen carefully and practice pronunciation as Japanese pronunciation is quite different from English.

def is_prime(n):

        if n % i == 0:

while count < 30:
    if is_prime(num):

        count += 1
